Linen

Linen is highly valued in CPHFW's sustainability standards for its low environmental impact and positive role in regenerative farming.

Flax requires little water or pesticides, but certified linen ensures sustainable processing and fair practices across the supply chain.

Using linen reflects a brand's commitment to durable, natural fibers and supports CPHFW's vision for sustainable fashion.
