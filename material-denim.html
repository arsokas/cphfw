Denim

Circular denim is a key sustainable material at CPHFW, emphasizing recycled content.

Brands must move away from conventional denim, using certified organic and high post-consumer recycled cotton to reduce water use and chemical impact.

CPHFW encourages monomaterial designs and innovations like Circulose® to ensure durability and future recyclability.
