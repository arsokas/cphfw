Denim

Circular Denim is a critical sustainable material showcased at Copenhagen Fashion Week (CPHFW), driven by mandatory requirements for recycled content.

Participating brands must transition away from conventional denim's resource-intensive production, prioritizing fabrics made from certified organic and high percentages of post-consumer recycled cotton. This shift supports supply chain transparency and significantly reduces the fashion industry's huge water consumption and chemical impact.

Ultimately, CPHFW promotes monomaterial design (like 100% cotton) and uses innovative materials like Circulose® to ensure denim is designed for longevity and future recyclability.
