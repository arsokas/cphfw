Viscoze

Viscose is a focus of CPHFW's sustainability standards due to its environmental risks, including toxic chemicals and deforestation.

To meet the 2025 target of 60% certified materials, brands should opt for sustainable alternatives like LENZING™ ECOVERO™ or TENCEL™ Lyocell.

These fibers use certified wood pulp and closed-loop systems, cutting chemical use and supporting responsible sourcing.
