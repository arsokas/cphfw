Wool

Wool is critical to the CPHFW Sustainability Requirements as brands must address both its environmental and social impacts, particularly concerning animal welfare.

While wool is a natural, renewable, and biodegradable fiber, CPHFW standards prioritize sourcing that adheres to rigorous third-party certifications like the Responsible Wool Standard (RWS). This certification ensures best practices in animal husbandry (e.g., no mulesing) and responsible land management to prevent overgrazing and protect biodiversity.

By demanding certified wool, Copenhagen Fashion Week encourages brands to demonstrate full supply chain traceability and uphold the highest standards for ethical and sustainable fashion materials.ent.
