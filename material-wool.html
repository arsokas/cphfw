Wool

Wool is central to CPHFW's sustainability goals, addressing both environmental and animal welfare concerns.

While renewable and biodegradable, it must meet standards like the Responsible Wool Standard (RWS), ensuring ethical treatment and sustainable land use.

Certified wool highlights traceability and reinforces CPHFW's commitment to ethical, sustainable fashion.
