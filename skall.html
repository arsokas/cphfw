SKALL

Skall Studio was founded in 2014 by sisters Julie and Marie Skall, long before responsibility became a buzzword in the fashion industry.

With a focus on integrity in all stages of production, collections without fur and leather, and classic styles in natural fibers that are built to last, Skall Studio is known as a values-based brand. Something that stems from a strong family history.
