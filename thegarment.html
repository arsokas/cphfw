THE.GARMENT

Designers Remix founder and designer Charlotte Eskildsen has collaborated with influencer Sophia Roe to create the new Danish fashion brand The Garment.

A brand that not unlike Charlotte Eskildsen's characteristic style will specialize in "closet heroes", as they call it. This involves a focus on the perfect suit, the white shirt, well-fitting dresses and other staples in a woman's wardrobe all made without seasons and with long-term use in mind.
