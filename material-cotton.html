Cotton

Cotton is a critical material in the context of Copenhagen Fashion Week (CPHFW) sustainability standards, driving brands toward responsible sourcing commitments. The CPHFW Action Plan mandates increasing the use of certified or preferred materials, which often includes organic and recycled cotton, to reduce the environmental footprint associated with traditional cotton farming (e.g., high water and pesticide use). Integrating sustainable cotton choices is essential for brands aiming for compliance, supply chain transparency, and achieving high-impact ESG fashion goals showcased at the event.
