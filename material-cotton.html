Cotton

Cotton is a key focus of CPHFW's sustainability standards, driving brands toward responsible sourcing.

The CPHFW Action Plan promotes certified or preferred materials—such as organic and recycled cotton—to lower cotton's environmental impact.

Using sustainable cotton is essential for compliance, transparency, and achieving strong ESG goals at Copenhagen Fashion Week.
